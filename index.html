Why do APOW?

Fun and Interesting Problems

I try my best to make these problems engaging and not boring. There's a lot of cool application questions and interesting physics ones.

Accessible to All Students

All students that are secondary or JC (or even lower) are welcome to try the problem set. You can do it at literally any time of the day!

Excellent Practice for Other Competitions

While most competitions are once a year, APOW is weekly and is excellent practice for major competitions like Astrigue, Astrochallenge, and of course SAO!

PRO TIPS

  • A lot of the non-calculation questions are not a direct recall, so you will have to break the question down. Often, you will need to use your brain to eliminate obvious fakes. This is a very important skill that comes with experience.
  • For the calculation questions, refer to your constant sheet and make sure your answer is logical.
  • APOWs are good practice for APOWs, so doing more sets will improve your scores in the future.
  • READ THE NOTES. There are tons of info if you know where to look, even if you lack knowledge in Astro.
